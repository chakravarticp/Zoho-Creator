Volunteer with AIESEC

AIESEC is a global platform for young people to develop their leadership potential through international internships and volunteer opportunities. Founded in 1948, AIESEC is a non-governmental, and not-for-profit organization entirely run by youth for youth.

Why Voluteer

Enhance your college education with practical and professional experiences. We provide a world of opportunities for you to have a unique entrepreneurial journey. We empower you to dream and think big as well as provide you the tools and resources to change it.

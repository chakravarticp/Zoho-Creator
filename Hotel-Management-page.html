About us

It is one of the fastest growing hostel chains in India that is uniquely designed, filled with activities & experiences for travellers to enjoy and offers a quality budget accommodation. In our stylish yellow-black themed hostels, we provide clean and safe housing facilities, secured lockers, well-stocked common kitchens, air conditioners, equipped libraries, exclusive female dorms, traveller’s cafes, chiller common rooms and wide open spaces where travellers can bond over cups of lip-smacking coffee. Additionally, all our hostels are equipped with services like laundry, visa facilitation, travel desk, etc. with eagerness to help the travellers 24/7.

Since hosting the 1st traveller in December, 2014, we have come a long way. Till date, The Hosteller has hosted 1,50,000+ travellers with more than 4,00,000 nights. Travellers, writers, photographers, young poets, comedians, solo travellers, female trekkers and what not, we have hosted them all. We take immense pride in providing quality hospitality to travellers from all over the globe and satisfaction when they review us online and offline in many ways.
